TransArmor® Solution & Annual Compliance Fee Residual Impacts
At the end of August, Ignite Payments kicked off the first phase of the expanded TransArmor® Solution Auto-Enrollment package. Existing merchants that did not opt out of the program by Sept. 30 were auto-enrolled in the TransArmor solution, and fee changes to the original Merchant Processing Agreement (MPA) went into effect. You may see changes to your October reciduals because of this.
For more information, view the full memo on MAO.
